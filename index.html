Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop/computer screen straight
  2. Keep yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the computer
  3. Move your right wrist up and down, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Now press the play button, and again move 3-4 feet away from the computer
  5. The red paddle is yours, and it will move according to the position of your right wrist!
